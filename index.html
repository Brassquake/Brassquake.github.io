About Brassquake

Founded in 2025, Brassquake filled with the finest brass musicians at Dr. G.W. Williams Secondary School. Our quintet brings together five dedicated students who share a passion for music and performance excellence.


The Brassquake Team at the GW Williams Music Banquet - (June 12th, 2025)

We specialize in a diverse repertoire ranging from classical works to modern arrangements, film scores, and popular music. Our goal is to showcase the versatility and power of brass instruments while inspiring our school community and beyond.
