About Brassquake

Founded in 2025, Brassquake represents the finest brass musicians at Dr. G.W. Williams Secondary School. Our quintet brings together five dedicated students who share a passion for brass music and performance excellence.


We specialize in a diverse repertoire ranging from classical brass quintet literature to modern arrangements, film scores, and popular music. Our goal is to showcase the versatility and power of brass instruments while inspiring our school community and beyond.


Through regular practice, performances, and musical collaboration, Brassquake continues to grow and develop as both individual musicians and as an ensemble, creating musical earthquakes wherever we perform!
